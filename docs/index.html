About

Grounded in Education.
Built on Curiosity.

Inland NW AI was founded on a simple belief: that AI adoption done right starts with genuine curiosity — not hype, not fear, and not pressure to keep up with the latest headline.

With a background in education and technology consulting, we've spent years helping organizations understand and adopt emerging technologies in ways that actually stick. Our work is practical, personalized, and grounded in how real teams learn and change.

We serve businesses, nonprofits, higher education institutions, and leadership teams across Spokane, Coeur d'Alene, and the broader Inland Northwest — who want to move thoughtfully, not just fast.
